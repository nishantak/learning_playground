Mathematical Explanation

Gradient descent updates a variable x using:

x ← x − η ∇f(x)

where η is the learning rate and ∇f(x) is the derivative.

Behavior:

  • Convex functions (like x²) converge to global minimum
  • Non-convex functions (like x⁴ − x²) have multiple minima
  • Result depends on initialization and learning rate
